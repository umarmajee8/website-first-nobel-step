Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Who is eligible to apply?
Any Pakistani national with a valid CNIC, a working email, and a WhatsApp number. Students, employed professionals, and registered founders are all welcome.
How long does the process take?
Your application reaches the advisory queue immediately. Initial feedback typically arrives within 3–5 working days for Basic, 48 hours for Standard, and within 24 hours for Professional and Entrepreneur members.
Which countries do you support?
The United States, United Kingdom, Canada, UAE and wider Gulf, Schengen Europe, and select Asia-Pacific destinations. If your target country isn't listed, pick "Other" in the application — many cases are handled bespoke.
What does the fee cover?
Your package fee covers the full advisory review, case handling, and platform access for the tier you selected. Any third-party fees (visa, exam, institution) are your own responsibility and are clearly called out upfront.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Data is transmitted over HTTPS, email is verified with a time-bound code, and your application is stored in access-controlled systems. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Can I upgrade my package later?
Absolutely. Many members start on Basic or Standard and upgrade once their case progresses. Write to support@firstnoblestep.com and we'll credit your earlier fee against the new tier.

Ready for your first nobel step?

Applications take about 10 minutes. No credit card needed for the free Basic tier.
